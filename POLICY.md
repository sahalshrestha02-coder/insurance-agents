# Insurance Policy Terms and Conditions

Welcome to our Insurance Company. This document outlines the terms and conditions of your insurance policy. 

## 1. Coverage Overview
Your policy provides coverage for specific risks as defined in your policy schedule. This may include:
- **Personal Liability**: Protection against legal liabilities for third-party injury or property damage.
- **Property Protection**: Coverage for accidental damage or loss to insured property.
- **Health & Life**: Specified benefits for medical emergencies or life-changing events.

## 2. Renewal Policy
- **Automated Reminders**: We use a multi-channel automated system (Email, WhatsApp, Voice) to notify you of upcoming renewals.
- **Renewal Thresholds**:
    - **30 Days**: Formal Email Notice.
    - **15 Days**: WhatsApp Reminder.
    - **7 Days**: Automated Voice Call.
    - **3 Days**: Human Intervention / Escalation.

## 3. Premium Payments
- Premiums must be paid by the renewal date to maintain continuous coverage.
- Failure to renew within the grace period may result in a lapse of coverage.

## 4. Claims Process
- All claims must be reported within 48 hours of the incident.
- Required documentation must be submitted via our secure portal.
