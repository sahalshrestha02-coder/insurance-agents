# Insurance Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## Claims Process
**Q: How long do I have to file a claim?**
A: All claims must be filed within 30 days of the incident. Documentation required includes the incident report, proof of loss, and relevant photos.

**Q: What is a deductible?**
A: A deductible is the amount you pay out-of-pocket before your insurance coverage kicks in. For example, if you have a $500 deductible and a $2,000 claim, you pay $500 and the insurance pays $1,500.

## Coverage Details
**Q: Is international travel covered?**
A: Standard policies cover travel within the continental United States. International riders can be added for an additional premium.

**Q: Does my policy cover rental cars?**
A: Yes, liability coverage extends to rental cars used for personal purposes. Comprehensive coverage for the rental vehicle itself depends on your specific plan level.

## Payments
**Q: Can I pay my premium in installments?**
A: Yes, we offer monthly, quarterly, and annual payment plans. Monthly plans incur a small convenience fee.

**Q: What happens if I miss a payment?**
A: We provide a 15-day grace period. If payment is not received within this period, your policy may be suspended.
